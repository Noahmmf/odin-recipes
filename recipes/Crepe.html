Crepe

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 tbls of sugar

Directions

  1. First, crack 3 eggs into a bowl.
  2. then add your cup of milk
  3. Once you have mixed the eggs and milk together, gradually mix in the flour until all mixed together.
  4. Add salt and sugar.
  5. Preheat the pan and use oil on pan.
  6. add 1 cup of mix to pan and swirl around till pan is covered.
  7. Cook for minute then flip Crepe to other side
